Project Overview: Real-Time Website Development for Rise Krishna Polytechnic College

During my 3rd year of B.Tech, I involved in the development of a real-time website for Rise Krishna Polytechnic College. The goal of the project was to create an interactive and informative platform that showcases the facilities and services offered by the college to students, parents, and other stakeholders. As part of my role in the project, I am responsible for gathering requirements, collecting relevant images, and designing the facilities screen.

Rise Krishna Polytechnic College website

The real-time website for Rise Krishna Polytechnic College aimed to provide a comprehensive online presence for the institution, allowing visitors to easily access information about the college, its facilities, academic programs, admission procedures, and other relevant details. The website served as a hub for prospective students and their parents to learn more about the college and make informed decisions.

In addition to gathering requirements, I am responsible for collecting high-quality images that depicted the various facilities available at the college. This involved collaborating with different departments, capturing photographs, and ensuring the visual representation accurately portrayed the college's infrastructure, labs, library, sports facilities, and other key areas.
